about

broeki is a Budapest-based indie dream-pop collaboration, blending soft textures, hazy melodies, and a slightly darker emotional undercurrent. Created as a space for intimate soundscapes and quiet intensity, broeki moves between warmth and shadow — always searching for the moment where nostalgia meets something new.
